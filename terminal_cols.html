Linux terminal columns

Number of terminal columns.

tput cols

Fill terminal line with A.

printf "\x41" | xargs yes 2>/dev/null | head -n $(tput cols) | tr -d "\n" | xargs printf "%s\n"

Fill terminal line with penguin. (Double the width of A.)

printf "\U1F427" | xargs yes 2>/dev/null | head -n $(($(tput cols)/2)) | tr -d "\n" | xargs printf "%s\n"

Fill line with penguin then next line with A.

printf "\x41" | xargs yes 2>/dev/null | head -n $(tput cols) | tr -d "\n" &&
printf "\U1F427" | xargs yes 2>/dev/null | head -n $(($(tput cols)/2)) | tr -d "\n" | xargs printf "%s\n"
